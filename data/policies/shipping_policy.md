# TechMart Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** May 15, 2025

## Shipping Methods and Costs

TechMart offers three shipping options for all domestic orders:

### Standard Shipping
- **Cost:** Free on orders over $50. Orders under $50 incur a flat $5.99 shipping fee.
- **Delivery Time:** 5-7 business days from the date of shipment.
- **Carrier:** USPS or UPS Ground, selected at TechMart's discretion.

### Express Shipping
- **Cost:** $12.99 flat rate regardless of order value.
- **Delivery Time:** 2-3 business days from the date of shipment.
- **Carrier:** UPS 2nd Day Air or FedEx Express Saver.

### Overnight Shipping
- **Cost:** $24.99 flat rate regardless of order value.
- **Delivery Time:** Next business day if ordered before 2:00 PM EST. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST ship the following business day.
- **Carrier:** FedEx Priority Overnight.
- **Restrictions:** Not available for PO Boxes. Not available for items over 50 lbs.

## Processing Time

All orders require 1-2 business days for processing before shipment. Processing includes payment verification, item picking, quality inspection, and packaging. During major sales events (Black Friday, Cyber Monday, end-of-year sales), processing time may extend to 3-4 business days.

## Order Tracking

Every shipment includes a tracking number sent via email within 24 hours of shipment. Customers can track orders through:
1. The "My Orders" section of their TechMart account.
2. The carrier's website using the tracking number provided.
3. Contacting TechMart customer support with their order number.

Tracking information may take up to 48 hours to become active after the tracking number is generated.

## Shipping Restrictions

- TechMart ships to all 50 US states and Washington DC.
- We do not currently ship internationally or to US territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, etc.).
- Lithium battery products (power banks, certain laptops) cannot be shipped via Overnight shipping due to carrier restrictions.
- Items weighing over 100 lbs require freight shipping. Contact customer support for a freight quote.

## Delivery Issues

### Lost Packages
If a package has not arrived within 3 business days after the estimated delivery date, customers should contact TechMart support. We will file a carrier investigation. If the package is confirmed lost, TechMart will either reship the order or issue a full refund at the customer's choice. Lost package claims must be filed within 30 days of the original shipment date.

### Damaged in Transit
If a package arrives visibly damaged, customers should photograph the damage before opening. Contact TechMart support within 7 days of delivery with photos. We will arrange a free return shipping label and either reship or refund the item.

### Wrong Address
TechMart is not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer. If a customer notices an address error before shipment, they should contact support immediately. After shipment, address changes are subject to a $10 redirect fee and may delay delivery by 2-3 additional business days.

## Holiday and Peak Season Shipping

During the holiday season (November 15 through December 31), all shipping estimates may be extended by 1-2 additional business days due to high carrier volume. TechMart recommends ordering by December 15 for Standard shipping or December 20 for Express shipping to ensure delivery before December 25.
