# TechMart Returns and Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** May 15, 2025

## Return Window

All items purchased from TechMart may be returned within **30 days** of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. After 30 days, returns are not accepted except for items covered under warranty.

## Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

1. **Unopened items:** Must be in original, sealed packaging. Full refund guaranteed.
2. **Opened items:** Must include all original accessories, manuals, and packaging. Items must show no signs of damage, misuse, or excessive wear. A **15% restocking fee** applies to opened items.
3. **Defective items:** Items that are defective or malfunctioning can be returned at any time within the 30-day window for a full refund with no restocking fee, regardless of whether they have been opened.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Gift cards and store credit
- Software licenses and digital downloads (once activated)
- Customized or personalized products
- Items marked as "Final Sale" on the product page
- Consumables (batteries, ink cartridges) that have been opened

## Refund Process

### Refund Methods
- **Original payment method:** Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after TechMart receives and inspects the returned item.
- **Store credit:** Customers may opt for store credit instead, which is issued within 24 hours and includes a **10% bonus** (e.g., a $100 refund becomes $110 in store credit).

### Refund Amounts
- Items returned in original sealed condition: **100% refund**
- Items returned opened but in good condition: **85% refund** (15% restocking fee)
- Defective items: **100% refund** regardless of condition
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a TechMart error (wrong item sent, defective product, etc.)

## Refund Thresholds and Approval

- Refunds under **$50** are auto-approved by the support system.
- Refunds between **$50 and $200** require supervisor review and approval within 24 hours.
- Refunds over **$200** require manager approval and may take up to 48 hours.
- All refunds regardless of amount are subject to verification of purchase and return condition.

## Exchange Policy

Exchanges are processed as a return of the original item plus a new order for the replacement:
- If the replacement costs **more**, the customer pays the difference.
- If the replacement costs **less**, the difference is refunded.
- Exchanges for the **same item** (e.g., different size/color) ship for free.
- Exchanges for a **different item** follow standard shipping rates.

## Return Shipping

- **Defective or wrong items:** TechMart provides a prepaid return label at no cost.
- **Change of mind returns:** The customer is responsible for return shipping costs. TechMart offers discounted return labels at $4.99 through our shipping partners.
- **In-store returns:** Not available — TechMart is an online-only retailer.

## Warranty Claims

TechMart products come with a **1-year manufacturer warranty** unless otherwise stated on the product page. Warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware malfunctions under normal use

Warranty does **not** cover:
- Physical damage (drops, spills, cracks)
- Software issues
- Unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Normal wear and tear

To file a warranty claim, contact TechMart support with your order number and a description of the issue. Warranty replacements are shipped within 3-5 business days after claim approval.
