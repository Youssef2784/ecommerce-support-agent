# TechMart Frequently Asked Questions

## Orders & Shipping

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and Overnight shipping delivers the next business day (if ordered before 2 PM EST). All orders require 1-2 business days for processing before shipment.

**Q: Is shipping free?**
A: Standard shipping is free on orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a flat $5.99 shipping fee. Express shipping is $12.99 and Overnight shipping is $24.99 regardless of order value.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also check the "My Orders" section in your TechMart account. Note that tracking information may take up to 48 hours to become active.

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
A: If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact support immediately and we can update the address at no charge. After shipment, address changes incur a $10 redirect fee and may delay delivery by 2-3 business days.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Currently, TechMart only ships within the 50 US states and Washington DC. We do not ship to US territories or international addresses.

## Returns & Refunds

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Unopened items receive a full refund. Opened items in good condition are subject to a 15% restocking fee. Defective items always receive a full refund regardless of condition.

**Q: How do I start a return?**
A: Contact TechMart customer support with your order number. We'll verify your eligibility and provide return instructions. For defective or wrong items, we'll email you a prepaid return label.

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Refunds under $50 are auto-approved. Refunds between $50-$200 require supervisor review (up to 24 hours). Refunds over $200 require manager approval (up to 48 hours).

**Q: Can I get store credit instead of a refund?**
A: Yes! If you choose store credit, it's issued within 24 hours and includes a 10% bonus. For example, a $100 refund becomes $110 in store credit.

**Q: What items can't be returned?**
A: Gift cards, activated software licenses, customized products, items marked "Final Sale," and opened consumables (batteries, ink) cannot be returned.

**Q: Who pays for return shipping?**
A: For defective or wrong items, TechMart provides a free prepaid return label. For change-of-mind returns, the customer pays for shipping. We offer discounted return labels at $4.99.

## Products

**Q: Are TechMart products covered by warranty?**
A: Yes, all products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty unless stated otherwise on the product page. Some accessories like the USB-C Hub and Mechanical Keyboard include a 2-year warranty. The Phone Case has a lifetime warranty.

**Q: What does the warranty cover?**
A: The warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions under normal use. It does not cover physical damage, software issues, unauthorized modifications, or normal wear and tear.

**Q: Can I use the Portable Charger on a plane?**
A: Yes, the 20,000mAh Portable Charger is under the 100Wh airline limit and is approved for carry-on luggage. Note: power banks must always be carried on, never checked.

**Q: Are the Wireless Earbuds waterproof?**
A: The Wireless Earbuds have an IPX5 rating, meaning they are water-resistant against splashes and sweat, but should not be submerged. The Bluetooth Speaker has a higher IPX7 rating and can be submerged in up to 1 meter of water for 30 minutes.

**Q: Does the USB-C Hub work with my laptop?**
A: The hub works with any device that has a USB-C port. However, video output (HDMI) requires the device's USB-C port to support DisplayPort Alt Mode. Most modern MacBooks, Windows ultrabooks, and iPad Pro models support this.

## Account & Payment

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: TechMart accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and TechMart store credit.

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Click "Sign Up" on the TechMart website and enter your email and a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Apple account.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, TechMart uses industry-standard SSL encryption and never stores your full credit card number. We are PCI DSS compliant.
